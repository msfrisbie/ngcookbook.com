AngularJS Web Application Development Cookbook

Matt Frisbie has been heavily involved in the AngularJS community for over two years. He first got involved by writing several free curricula for AngularJS beginners, which were a smash hit.

Although AngularJS has been kind to him, he classifies himself as a full-stack engineer with experience in Node, Rails, and Django. He is a 2012 graduate of the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign in computer engineering.

He has primarily worked as an early startup engineer, and has significant experience in the education technology, open knowledge, and delivery logistics industries.

In addition to publishing the book, he also authored an AngularJS video series, does webcasts for O'Reilly Media, and speaks at frontend developer meetups.
